Strawberry_cakes



Ingredients

  • 100g white chocolate
  • 100g gariguette strawberries
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g of softened butter
  • 140g flour
  • ½ sachet of baking powder
  • 180g brown sugar

Preparation steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 ° C.
  2. Beat the softened butter with the brown sugar.
  3. Add the eggs to the previously beaten, mix well.
  4. Melt the chocolate in a bain-marie then incorporate it into the appliance.
  5. Cut the strawberries into cubes.
  6. Add the yeast with the flour, mix well until obtaining a smooth dough.
  7. Stir in the strawberries.
  8. Pour the appliance into a buttered mold.
  9. Bake for approx 35 / 40min.
  10. Let cool and unmold.
  11. Cut into small square portions and sprinkle with icing sugar.

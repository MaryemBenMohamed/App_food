Kiwi_juice



Ingredients

  • 3 kiwi
  • 4-5 ice cubes or a handful
  • 1.5 tsp sugar or honey
  • 1/2 cup water

Preparation steps


  1. Peel the kiwi fruit well with the help of a peeler.
  2. Make small slices of the fruit with a knife.
  3. Add the slices of the kiwi into a juice blender.
  4. Add ice cubes, sugar/honey, little water.
  5. Blend the juice finely.
  6. Take serving glasses and pour the juice directly without straining.
  7. Garnish the juice with round slices of kiwi over the juice.
  8. Serve the juice chilled and fresh.
